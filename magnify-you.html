Why the Magnify You Chapter Program?

Create Impact

Skip the planning process for a completely new organization and get right to becoming a founder

Exclusive resources

With our large social capital, you can gain access to our exclusive partnership and events

Recognition & Awards

Receive the Mental Health Hero of the Month award based on your chapter’s efforts Gain personalized mentorship sessions from high-achieving, accomplished STEAM mentors

Connection

Personal Growth

Learn about key psychological facts, self-care tips, and much more from the experts!
